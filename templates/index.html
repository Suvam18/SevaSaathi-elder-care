Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using SevaSaathi for your loved ones.

Is SevaSaathi easy for seniors to use?

Yes! We designed the app specifically for older adults with large fonts, high contrast colors, and simplified menus to reduce confusion.

Can family members access the health data?

Absolutely. With our Premium plan, you can link a Caregiver Dashboard where family members can monitor vitals and receive alerts.

How does the SOS button work?

Pressing the SOS button instantly sends an automated phone call, SMS, and app notification with GPS coordinates to all registered emergency contacts.

Do I need special hardware?

No, SevaSaathi works entirely on your existing smartphone. No extra pendants or base stations are required.
